# Daniel and Friends (但以理和他的朋友们) 🦁✨

Welcome to our little corner! 

## The Story Behind 💭

One day, a thought suddenly popped into my mind: wouldn't it be wonderful to have a little widget, just like Duolingo, but instead of reminding you to practice a language, it greets you with a fresh, encouraging Bible verse every single day? 

And just like that, **Daniel and Friends** was born! It’s a small passion project designed to bring a little bit of light and warmth to your daily routine. ☀️

## What's Inside? 🏰

Here's a peek at how this little app works:

*   **Daily Verses Widget**: The heart of the app! A home screen widget that refreshes daily to show you a new verse.
*   **Midnight Magic 🌙**: A smart background update system (MidnightUpdateManager) ensures that a new verse is ready and waiting for you right when you wake up.
*   **Global Friends 🌍**: Supports Chinese, English, and Korean, so friends from different places can enjoy it too.
*   **Built With**: SwiftUI & Firebase.

## Architecture Overview 🛠️

The project is built using:
*   **SwiftUI**: For a beautiful and responsive user interface.
*   **WidgetKit**: To bring the verses directly to your home screen.
*   **Firebase**: For backend support and data management.
*   **Core Data / UserDefaults**: To keep your preferences and daily verses safe locally.
